About Casa Buildia

CASA BUILDIA DEVELOPMENT CORPORATION is a corporate entity that will cater in line with construction business that will serve to private entities and government sectors anywhere in the Philippines. The company offers a design and build construction development of any type of the establishments with quality service, high-standard deliverable in a construction industry.

Engr. Von Lloyd Gee N. Ocenar and Crestina O. Ocenar, are the pioneers behind Casa Buildia Development Corporation who ventures a construction business that will design, build, and establish projects anywhere in the Philippines.

Our people are our greatest asset. The construction industry, in general, is notorious for high turnover of project, not just in design but the concept and the totality of high-end and quality but in reasonable cost of value. The Casa Buildia Development Corporation has admin workers and accredited sub-contractors who have been with us since day one.
